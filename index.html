2. Traditional Struggles

Elderly patients with severe arthritis, joint pain, or stroke survivors cannot open child-proof caps or align small lids because these tasks demand two hands.

The Problem

Pinching and twisting require two active hands.

3. The Rotadex Solution

Rotadex converts fine motor finger movements into a single, effortless downward palm press. The device stays stable on the table throughout the motion.

The Solution

Gross-motor palm push triggers automatic rotation.

4. Ratchet & Pawl System

The internal mechanics utilize a 7-tooth ratchet and indexing pawl arm. This converts the vertical press into a 51.4-degree carousel indexing movement.

Design Specifications:

• 11 Component Parts
• 8,664 Total Triangles in Mesh
• Helical return spring with 5 active coils

5. Ergonomic Scoop Retrieval

Retrieval is guided by a curved scoop dispenser cup. Instead of picking up individual pills, the user sweeps their hand in a natural curved motion.

Cup Dimension:

• 44 mm width to accommodate tremors
• Low curved front wall acts as a ramp

6. Key Engineering Features

Our design is mechanical, robust, and cost-effective. Key features include:

✓ Independent One-Handed Operation
✓ Heavy Base Plate to Prevent Tipping
✓ Anti-Reverse Latch (Prevents Double Dose)
✓ Auto-Spring Reset Plunger System
